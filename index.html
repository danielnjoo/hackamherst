FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Students come together to build cool software & hardware hacks over 24-48 hours. Itâ€™s very beginner friendly.

Who is allowed to participate?

Anyone who's a current student: registration at the event will require presentation of a valid student ID.

What are the sleeping arrangements?

Subject to later confirmation, a place for sleeping will be provided. If you are not an Amherst College student, we recommend bringing a sleeping bag as our own supply may be limited.

Do I need to be a CS major or need to know how to code?

Certainly not! In fact, we expressedly encourage non-CS majors to explore hacking! And if you have no experience coding, then our pre-hack schedule is for you.

Do I need a team?

You can work in a group of up to 4, and don't worry if you don't come with one / a fully-formed one -- there'll be time for that at the event!
