Day of Information

The Theme

The theme for this hackathon is improving student life, so come ready to fix all those things you and your friends complain about!

Prizes

(1) $500 grand prize and (2) $250 runner-up prizes. In order to be eligible for the prizes, you must present at the end.

Judging Criteria

  • Quality: What was the idea?
  • Technology: What technologies did the project use? Did it use them well?
  • Completeness: How polished and ready to use is the project?

Teams

Teams of up to 4, no rules/restrictions. Come with a team or form one once you arrive, there's no explicit time for team formation, so if you don’t have a team it’s best to arrive early.
